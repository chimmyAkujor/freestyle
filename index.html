THE DEVIL'S TRIANGLE

The bermuda Triangle aka the devil's triangle is an area in the atlantic ocean that
has its imaginary points located at Florida, Puerto Rico and the Bermuda Islands.
Hundreds of aircrafts and ships have vanished in this area without any trace.
